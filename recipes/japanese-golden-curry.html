Japanese Golden Curry Recipe

Description

A nice warm meal made with vegetables and either chicken or tofu. The sauce is S&B Golden Curry Sauce which is sweet and salty and pairs well with the ingredients. Make sure to include fresh, hot rice on the side to complete this delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • 1 Boneless and skinless chicken thighs OR Tofu
  • 2-3 cubes of S&B Golden Curry
  • Water
  • 1 Medium Potato
  • 1 Medium Carrot

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil on the pan: Make sure the oil is hot enough. You should feel the heat when your float your hand is above the pan.
  2. Add proteins to pan:Place chopped chicken/tofu cubes onto your pan and cook until it is brown on all sides
  3. Add water
  4. Add vegetables: sliced carrots and cubed potatoes onto pan
  5. Boil water: Wait until water is boiling
  6. Lower heat: Lower heat until ingredients simmer
  7. Simmer for 25 minutes: Wait until vegetables soften
  8. Add in S&B golden curry cubes
  9. Mix curry for 5 minutes: Mix until sauce and water are mixed well
