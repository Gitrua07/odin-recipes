Vegetable and Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Description

The vegetable and egg fried rice is an easy meal to make. The meal is made out of rice, vegetables, and egg. It is good to eat for lunch or dinner. You need few ingredients, and is inexpensive. You only need a pan and a stove.

ingredients

  • Frozen Mixed Vegetables
  • Eggs
  • Jasmine Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Ground Black Pepper
