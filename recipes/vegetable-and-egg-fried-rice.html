Vegetable and Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Description

The vegetable and egg fried rice is an easy meal to make. The meal is made out of rice, vegetables, and egg. It is good to eat for lunch or dinner. You need few ingredients, and is inexpensive. You only need a pan and a stove.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Mixed Vegetables
  • Eggs
  • Jasmine Rice
  • Soy Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil: Pour some olive oil on the frying pan and wait until feels hot enough. Or it begins frying when you place the ingredients on it.
  2. Add the mixed vegetables
  3. Add the seasoning on the vegetables: Add in the following seasoning...
    • Garlic Powder
    • Onion Powder
    • Ground Black Pepper
  4. Mix the seasoning and spice: Mix for a few minutes until you can smell the seasoning well.
  5. Add in the beaten eggs: Create a hole in the middle of the vegetables and pour in the beaten eggs.
  6. Wait for the eggs to cook before mixing: It should take a few minutes until egg is solid. Scramble while waiting for egg to cook.
  7. Mix eggs and vegetables together: Mix until fully combined.
  8. Add rice and soy sauce: In tiny chunks, add rice and soy sauce together at the same time.
  9. Mix everything together: Make sure everything is well mixed. This should take around 5 minutes.
